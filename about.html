Core Belief

The why, how, and what.

Everything WOW does flows from a single conviction. Here's the thinking behind it.

Why

No one becomes their bravest self alone.

Growth has never been a solo endeavour. The most significant shifts happen in the company of others — people who challenge you, reflect your thinking back, and remind you of what's possible. That's the foundation of everything WOW does.

How

By creating a strategic pause.

Strong women don't need fixing. They need space to think, laugh, reflect, and tell the truth. Away from noise, pressure, and expectation, clarity emerges. With the right people around them, that clarity turns into action.

What

A connected global community.

Coaching circles, retreats, workshops, masterclasses — and a network of women across the world who stay connected long after the programme ends. WOW is not just a programme. It's a force.

Our story

Built for women who are ready for what's next.

WOW was founded by Isa Buencamino-Agbayani, a Filipino leadership coach based between England and Bahrain, with 29 years of corporate leadership development experience. She saw a consistent pattern: ambitious, capable women with experience and drive — stalling not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked space.

Space to think clearly. Space to be honest. Space to be held accountable by people who genuinely understood the weight of building a life and a career at the same time.

Strong women don't need fixing. They need space to think, laugh, reflect, and tell the truth.

WOW was built to create that space. What began as a structured group coaching programme has grown into a global community — built on rigorous coaching practice, and held together by something that surprises many participants: the relationships they didn't expect to form.

Strangers become sisters. That's not a tagline. It's what actually happens.

Our values

What holds
WOW together.

01

Courage

"Courage grows faster together."

Courage is the shift WOW creates. People leave clearer about what matters and braver about acting on it. It doesn't happen in isolation — it builds in the company of others willing to be honest.

02

Kindness

"Steady, not soft."

Kindness shapes every WOW interaction. It's how people are held, challenged, and supported. WOW leads with heart, care, and humanity — even when conversations are hard.

03

Sisterhood

"Strangers become sisters."

WOW grows stronger in community. People gain momentum when they're seen, supported, and connected. What begins as a group becomes a sisterhood — and that sisterhood lasts.

The founder

"Leadership development is about helping women see what's already possible — and giving them the room to act on it."

Isa Buencamino-Agbayani Founder · WOW Coaching · ICF Accredited Coach (PCC)

Isa is a Filipino leadership coach based between England and Bahrain. She began her career working for large multinational organisations before setting up her own company in 2018 in the UK. With 29 years of corporate leadership development experience — including senior roles at Unilever and Interserve — she has coached senior executives across Europe, Asia, South America, and Africa.

An ICF-accredited PCC coach and Associate Faculty of the Teleos Leadership Institute in Philadelphia, Isa also leads Coaching Essentials International, an ICF accreditation programme. She founded WOW in 2020 from a simple conviction: that the most ambitious women in the world needed each other more than they needed another solo coaching programme.
