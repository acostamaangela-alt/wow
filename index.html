About WOW

For ambitious women shaping what's next.

The Women of the World (WOW) coaching programme will empower you to go beyond your expectations of yourself, make the most of what you have already achieved, and let you live your best life.

The programme has helped women from all around the world. You'll be joined by other women on their own path to success — a stimulating international learning experience that gives you fresh, diverse perspectives and insights to use in your personal and professional life.

Outcomes our clients have achieved

  • Getting the promotion they always wanted
  • Moving companies and countries
  • Starting their own company
  • Restructuring work to prioritise work-life balance
  • Changing jobs or careers

Why WOW works

Three things you'll walk away with.

01

Global insights & support

From experienced and successful coaches who understand your career journey and aspirations — and from a peer group of women across the world.

02

Clarity & an action plan

Clarify your objectives and create a step-by-step action plan in a supportive and encouraging environment, grounded in your priorities and drivers.

03

Meaningful relationships

Foster real connections with women from diverse communities — an inspiring network that continues to support you through the alumni network and events.

Masterclasses

Continue learning with your WOW network.

Focussed one-hour sessions, delivered monthly, providing additional insight and depth on key development topics.

Masterclasses include structured 'teach-ins', keynote sessions from recognised specialists, and conversations with inspiring female role models.

They provide an extra dimension to your learning while you're on your WOW coaching programme. Afterwards, continuing to attend Masterclasses ensures your ongoing development — keeping you connected to and supported by the WOW network.

  • Structured teach-ins on development areas
  • Keynote sessions from recognised specialists
  • Conversations with inspiring female role models
