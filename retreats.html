A purposeful pause,
redesigned.

WOW Retreats are immersive experiences created for women seeking clarity, connection, and direction. Set in carefully chosen locations around the world, each retreat is designed as a strategic pause — a space to step away from daily demands and reconnect with what matters most.

Through coaching, guided conversations, and shared experiences in nature, WOW Retreats support reflection, insight, and meaningful personal growth. Every retreat is intentionally small, deeply considered, and led by experienced WOW coaches.

Herefordshire, England.

May 29 – June 1, 2026 4 days, 3 nights Shucknall Court

Set in the English countryside, this retreat is a return to where WOW Coaching was first conceived. Herefordshire's gentle landscapes, ancient pathways, and quiet strength create an environment that invites presence over performance and depth over distraction.

Why England

In many ways, this retreat is a return — to the quiet landscapes where WOW Coaching was first conceived. The idea for WOW took shape while walking through the English countryside, a place that invites space to think, breathe, and listen to what truly matters.

England offers no shortage of beautiful places. We chose Herefordshire for its gentle landscapes, ancient pathways, orchards, and quiet strength — a place that invites presence rather than performance, depth rather than distraction.

What you'll gain

You'll leave with greater clarity, renewed energy, and a deeper sense of direction, along with a community of women who will continue to inspire and support you beyond the retreat.

Designed as a four-day strategic pause, the England retreat offers space to reflect, reframe, and reconnect — supported by coaching, guided dialogue, and shared time in nature.

Retreat 02 · Upcoming

Komodo, Indonesia.

August 21 – 24, 2026 4 days, 3 nights Labuan Bajo

Set in the wild beauty of Labuan Bajo, gateway to the Komodo region of Indonesia, this retreat takes us to the edge of land and sea. Rugged islands, vast horizons, and luminous waters create an environment that invites courage over comfort and connection over convention.

Why Komodo

Finding the right retreat location in Indonesia has always mattered. This will be our second WOW Retreat in Komodo, and returning there feels intentional. The first experience confirmed what we sensed from the start — this place naturally creates the conditions for reflection, connection, and perspective.

Indonesia offers many extraordinary destinations — Bali, Lombok, and the islands of Flores among them. But if you could experience just one place, where would nature most powerfully invite you to pause and reconnect? That place is Komodo National Park.

Not only for the rare encounter with the Komodo dragons, but for the experience as a whole — sailing between islands, swimming in clear waters, and being surrounded by a landscape and culture that feels raw, real, and grounding.

What you'll gain

You'll leave with greater clarity, renewed energy, and a deeper sense of direction, along with a community of women who will continue to inspire and support you beyond the retreat.

Step out of the everyday — and into a setting that creates space for perspective, reflection, and meaningful connection.

Retreat 03 · Upcoming

El Nido, Palawan.

January 8 – 11, 2027 4 days, 3 nights El Nido, Philippines

Set in the breathtaking seascapes of El Nido, Palawan, this retreat unfolds among limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and waters of impossible clarity. There is something both powerful and gentle about this landscape — dramatic in form, yet deeply calming in presence.

Why Palawan

Finding the right retreat location in the Philippines was deeply personal for us. It is the WOW Retreat in our home country, and we felt a responsibility — not just to get it right, but to honour the richness and beauty of the Philippines in a way that felt true.

There are so many stunning places to choose from — like Siargao and Bohol and even the lesser-known but equally breathtaking Sorsogon. We have visited all of them, hoping the right one would reveal itself.

But in the end, we asked ourselves a simple question: if one could visit the Philippines only once, where would they go that captures both its natural wonder and culture? Palawan kept rising to the top. So it became clear: for the retreat in the Philippines, it had to be Palawan. It's our love letter to the country — and to the women we'll be welcoming there.

What you'll gain

Here, surrounded by nature's artistry, we are invited to soften and expand at the same time. To release what no longer serves, to rediscover what feels true, and to reconnect with the quiet wisdom within.

In a setting shaped by water, light, and time, depth comes naturally — and transformation feels both grounded and free. You'll leave with greater clarity, renewed energy, and a community of women who will continue to inspire and support you beyond the retreat.
